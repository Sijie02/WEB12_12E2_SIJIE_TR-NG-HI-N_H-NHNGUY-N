ITALIAN CUISINE

Known for fresh ingredients and regional diversity.
Pizza Margherita: A vibrant, balanced blend of acidic, creamy, and herbal flavors.
Pasta Carbonara: A silky, rich sauce of egg, cheese, and guanciale coats the pasta.
Risotto alla Milanese: A creamy, saffron-infused rice with a luxurious, wavy consistency.

CHINESE CUISINE

A tapestry of flavors and textures.
Peking Duck: A crispy-skinned duck served with pancakes and savory accompaniments.
Dim Sum: Shanghai Soup Dumplings (Xiǎo Lóng Bāo): Dumplings with a rich, hot broth inside a delicate, steamed pouch.
Hot Pot: Sichuan Ma La Hot Pot: A fiery, numbing broth for cooking a variety of meats and vegetables.

FRENCH CUISINE

Renowned for its elegance, sophistication, and emphasis on sauces, pastries, and fine wine.
Croissant: A French Viennoiserie in a crescent shape made from a laminated yeast dough that sits between a bread and a puff pastry.
Steak Tartare: A French dish of raw ground beef. It is usually served with onions, capers, parsley or chive, salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce and other seasonings.
Crème Brûlée: A French dessert consisting of a rich custard base topped with a layer of hardened caramelized sugar.

JAPANESE CUISINE

INDIAN CUISINE
